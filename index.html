Our Firm

Spear Capital & Advisory (SCA) is a wholly owned Ghanaian management consulting firm setup to achieve the sole purpose of helping the following enterprises upscale their businesses to achieve their growth and financial aspirations:

  • Small office/ Home office Enterprises.
  • Sole Proprietorships.
  • Small & Medium Enterprises.

Working together with our clients, we aspire to present the best possible solutions, outlining the opportunities available while considering the inherent risks. Our strategic growth plans are cognisant of the dynamics within the client industry; therefore are fully agile, allowing our clients to execute achieving the highest impact possible.

Corporate & Financial Advisory

At Spear Capital & Advisory, we appreciate that it may be difficult for our clients to have a partner that provides prudent corporate advice on navigating the industry while having a firm grip on the sound financial management.

  • Therefore, as a key pillar in our service offering, we leverage on our knowledge of the financial markets and global economy with proven research to present the best advisory ideas to our clients.
  • Additionally, where there is the need for injection of capital into the business, we scout for the best possible credit facilities under favourable terms for the client. That way, we don’t leave the client half way through the journey hoping they’re able to get to the finish line.

What we seek to do at Spear Capital & Advisory, is provide a roadmap on achieving their business objectives despite the headwinds experienced.

Corporate Strategy

Achieving a high level of growth within any industry requires a deliberate plan built to deliver sustainable results for long term. Many SoHo & SME businesses begin with a solution for a problem within a small context. However, as the business grows, tact is required especially when scalability of solutions is brought into the discussion.

  • We deliver to our clients, scalable, agile and goal oriented corporate strategies that fully incorporate all the requirements of a corporate strategic plan.
  • We consider the strengths and weaknesses of the business, the possible risks internal and external, then fully discuss the outcomes with the client.
  • We incorporate relevant data to support our corporate strategic plan to show possible results that are relatable to the client.

Once agreed, we develop a project plan taking into account all the necessary risks to deliver this strategy with the desired results.

Project Management

At Spear Capital & Advisory, we pride ourselves in being a technically adept yet versatile firm, capable of guiding and delivering on business projects for small office/ home office, sole proprietorships and small & medium scale enterprises.

  • We incorporate all the corporate strategic plans of our clients into project road map.
  • Discuss with the client, sign off and begin implementation ensuring to follow the tenets of the Project Management Profession.
  • We also acknowledge that, not all problems will require a full scale project management approach. In such instances, we design a road map fit for purpose to achieve the set objectives of the client.

Our Project Management team comprises of partners who have experience and have proven to trustworthy in delivering sustainable project outcomes to our clients. We believe that our client’s journeys to success may not be as simple as drawing up project related plans but we believe in the Ubuntu spirit which says, “Together we can” and together we will work to deliver the objectives our clients.
